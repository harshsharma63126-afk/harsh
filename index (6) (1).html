Our Story

Built by a Jaipur family that simply loves driving.

Rajasthan Self Drive started when my younger brother asked, half-joking, why everyone in Jaipur had to rent a driver along with a car. We were the family that drove ourselves to Pushkar at sunrise, to Nahargarh for chai, to Sariska just because the weekend was free. Renting a car shouldn't change that.

So we put together a small, hand-picked fleet — every car we'd actually take our own parents in — and ran it out of Pratap Nagar. No commission middlemen. No surprises at handover. Just a clean car, a quick KYC, and the keys.

Today the cars range from a Swift to a Thar, but the rule hasn't changed: if it isn't road-trip ready on a Friday morning, it doesn't leave the lot.

  • Every car serviced, sanitised and fuel-checked before handover
  • Free pickup & drop within Pratap Nagar & nearby sectors
  • 24×7 roadside assistance — a real human picks up
  • Transparent rates, no surprise kilometre traps
  • Outstation packages for Pushkar, Udaipur, Ranthambore & Mount Abu
